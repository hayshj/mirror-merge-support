Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restart a game?

From the Game Over screen, tap Play Again. You can also restart from the settings (gear) menu while playing.

Why am I seeing ads?

Mirror Merge is supported by ads. Ads help keep the app free to download and maintain.

Why aren’t ads showing?

Ads may not appear every time. They can also be affected by your network connection or ad availability in your region.

My high score disappeared — why?

High scores are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear its storage, your saved score may be removed.

I found a bug. What information should I include?

Please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your Android/iOS version
  • The app version (shown in the store listing)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • A screenshot or screen recording (if possible)
